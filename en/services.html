Functional Agricultural Materials

Smart Agricultural & Fresh-keeping Materials

Through a deep understanding of plant physiological metabolic pathways, the Fort technical team has developed targeted biological regulation promoters and natural protective agents that meet the needs of the fresh food processing and export industry.

Photosynthesis Promoter Fertilizer Under Construction

Using exclusive trace metal coordination chelates, spraying can briefly adjust leaf cell osmotic pressure and spectral capture range, stimulating key carbon-fixing enzymes like Rubisco. Currently, this product formula is in the field trial and operational SOP calibration phase. Relevant comparative test data regarding SPAD chlorophyll relative values and average yield increases are being compiled and will not be disclosed before official release.

Cut Anti-Mold Preservative In Experiment, Compiling Results

A food-grade natural protective coating specifically developed for pineapple exports. Targeting the paint point where the stem cuts are highly susceptible to mold infections due to drastic temperature fluctuations inside shipping containers during sea transport, applying it to the stem cuts forms a stable micro/nano-scale protective barrier, effectively blocking gray mold and blue mold. Currently, this preservative is in the multi-group comparative experiment phase. Detailed test data on stem mold inhibition rates, sea freight shelf life extension, and transoceanic loss reduction rates are being compiled and will not be disclosed before official release.

Sustainable Materials & Chemicals (Materials & Chemicals)

丰泰技研將材料化學技術延伸應用於精密組件重工製程，開發出「安全無損解UV膠劑」與「安全無損解熱固膠劑」。排除傳統強酸鹼與剧毒氯系溶劑（二氯甲烷），以溫和滲透膨潤機制，解決高價值電子廢棄物重工的回收痛點。

  • "Swelling-Peeling" Synergistic Mechanism：Guides solvent molecules into the high-strength cured cross-linked network, swelling the volume and reducing adhesion, allowing it to be easily peeled off in sheets without damaging PC, optical glass, or gold wires.
